About

Hello, my name is MinerCat36 and I am a software developer among other things. I have always loved tech and got into coding in middle school, my first text-based language being python. I love learning, and because of this, I have other hobbies like photography, music (violin), scuba diving, and making. I always have a desire to take on a challenge no matter how difficult, and never stop learning :)

While learning my first language, Python in 6th grade I did many projects, none particularly good but it got me started. I continued learning throughout 6th grade and the summer, wading through the neverending amount of bugs and errors I got but eventually, I began to create interesting stuff. During this time Stack Overflow, Google and Youtube were my friends and in 7th grade, I began to learn other languages like C# for Unity and Javascript for web development. You can see all of my projects and growth in the Gallery. I have yet to see all of software development and this is just the tip of what is out there but I will always have an undying passion to keep learning, usually by messing around with stuff like Rasberry Pis, Microbits, and Little Bits but also by researching what I am curious about.
